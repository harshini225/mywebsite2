nausea,
screenprint on paper,

waiting for godot,
screenprint on paper,
2022

this project places fish into scenes from my favorite Western existentialist and absurdist stories. from left to right: Metamorphosis by Franz Kafka, Nausea by Jean-Paul Sartre, and Waiting for Godot by Samuel Beckett. i hoped to convey the same feeling that i felt as i read and felt myself in these works through bookplate-style interpretations.

bookmaking: desert, living book

this book was created during a RISD course called Letterpress and Non-Stitch Bookbinding. it uses letterpress printing, drum leaf binding, and pop-up/paper engineering techniques to depict life in the desert, which is often mischaracterized as lifeless and barren.
